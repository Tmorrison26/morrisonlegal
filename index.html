    Expert Legal Services

    ESTATE PLANNING

    We provide expert legal services in all areas of Estate Planning. Based upon the wishes of our clients, the proper estate plan can allow our clients to enjoy life, knowing their affairs are in order and provide clear guidance to family members that have lost a loved one.

    The documents we draft include Wills, Advanced Directive for Health Care, Power of Attorney, and various trusts

    PROBATE

    The loss of a loved one is a stressful and difficult time for those left behind. We will work with your family to negotiate the obstacles of probating the estate and distributing the assets. We provide guidance on the steps that need to be taken and effectively communicate with the family throughout the process.
