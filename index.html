    Experienced Legal Guidance

    At Morrison Legal, LLC, I pride myself on providing experienced legal guidance tailored to your specific needs. The documents provided in your Estate Plan will be customized to meet your desired outcome. I will help you navigate through complex legal issues with confidence.
    Expert Legal Services

    ESTATE PLANNING

    We provide expert legal services in all areas of Estate Planning. Based upon the wishes of our clients, the proper estate plan can allow our clients to enjoy life, knowing their affairs are in order and provide clear guidance to family members that have lost a loved one.

    The documents we draft include Wills, Advanced Directive for Health Care, Power of Attorney, and various trusts

    PROBATE

    The loss of a loved one is a stressful and difficult time for those left behind. We will work with your family to negotiate the obstacles of probating the estate and distributing the assets. We provide guidance on the steps that need to be taken and effectively communicate with the family throughout the process.

    PERSONAL INJURY

    If you have been injured due to someone else's negligence, our attorneys can help you recover the compensation you deserve.

    REAL ESTATE LAW

    Our attorneys have experience representing clients in real estate matters. We handle everything from simple residential transfers to actions to quiet title.

    Why Choose Morrison Legal?

    PERSONALIZED ATTENTION

    At Morrison Legal, we provide personalized attention to each of our clients. We recognize each person has a unique situation. We take the time to understand the details of your specific circumstances and develop a legal strategy tailored to your needs.

    CLIENT FOCUSED RESULTS

    Our experienced attorney has a proven track record of success. We have helped numerous clients achieve their legal goals. Through personalized interaction with our clients and clear communication, we are able to understand the expectations of our clients and provide them a strategy to best meet the desired result. We are confident we can do the same for you.

    AFFORDABLE RATES

    While cost is always a concern, we believe quality legal representation should be accessible to everyone, regardless of their financial situation. That's why we offer affordable rates and flexible payment plans.

    FLEXIBLE HOURS

    We understand our clients are busy. It can be difficult to make appointments when other responsibilities and activities cannot be rearranged. To assist in relieving this burden, we work flexible hours to allow us to meet with clients on their schedule. We can schedule appointments to meet in the evenings and weekends, to accommodate a hectic schedule.

    HOUSE CALLS

    We make house calls. For the convenience of our clients, we can meet our clients at a location of their choosing. We hope these accommodations make our services more accessible and remove the limitations placed on clients by age, disability, scheduling conflicts, or responsibilities.

    DIRECT ACCESS

    The contact information provided to our clients is for direct access to the attorney.

    Meet Our Attorney

    LARRY W. MORRISON

    I am a graduate of the Robert H. McKinney School of Law in Indianapolis, Indiana. I obtained my undergraduate degree in Criminal Justice from Indiana University Purdue University in Indianapolis, Indiana.

    I have worked in public service since I graduated high school. I served in the United States Marine Corps for four years before being hired by the State of Indiana. I worked for the Indiana Department of Natural Resources as a Conservation Officer for 25 years.

    I decided to enter the practice of law at a later age then most attorneys. Throughout my life, I observed several situations and people in various circumstances who were in need of legal advice but could not afford to get it. I was in a position to attend school and if I could accomplish my goal, I believed I could use my degree and experience to assist others in similar situations. My wife and I are both dedicated to helping those who are willing to help themselves, but may not have the means or the method required.
    While Morrison Legal is a manner of making a living, it is much more than that. It is the mechanism I can use to assist those less fortunate or needing guidance in the right direction. My faith in God and how He has guided my path to put me in this position is beyond what I could have asked for.

    I am here to work for you and provide you the best legal services I can.
